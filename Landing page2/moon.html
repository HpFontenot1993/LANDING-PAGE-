what is the Moon?

The Moon is a Satellite Planet. The Moon's job for earth is to: Keep us on axis, keep our feet on the ground with its gravity. The Moon was also the first planet humans were able to land on.

Here are the MAIN points!

  • The moons full orbit is 27 earth days.
  • The moon is made of 3 sections. 1. core 2. mantle 3. crust
  • Due to its gravity pull, the moon causes the tides in our oceans.
