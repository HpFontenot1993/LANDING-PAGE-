What is the Sun?

The Sun is a large Star. Which like other Stars are MOSTLY made up of gas. The Sun's role to earth is to help maintian its tempature. It also assists its habitants with their natural sleep cycles and habitual patterns. Without the Sun our planet would freeze over thus causing us to cease to exist.


Fun facts about our Sun!

  • Made of: Hydrogen, Helium and it's own gravity.
  • 7 sections: 1. Core 2. Radiative Zone 3.Convection Zone. 4. Photosphere 5. Chromosphere 6. Transition Zone 7. Corona
  • The Sun's Magnetic feild extendes past our Solar System.
